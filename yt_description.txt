GitHub: https://github.com/natalia-jaskowska/guardianlens

GuardianLens watches a child's screen in real time, detects grooming,
cyberbullying, and inappropriate content using a local Gemma 4 vision model —
and alerts the parent instantly. No chat data ever leaves the device.

Other apps count minutes. GuardianLens understands what's happening.

Built for the Gemma 4 Good Hackathon (Kaggle × Google DeepMind) —
Safety & Trust track.

HOW IT WORKS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
• Captures the child's screen every 15 seconds
• Gemma 4 vision model reads visible chat text and identifies the platform
• Structured AI reasoning: extract conversations → assess threat → generate alert
• Live dashboard streams status and the model's full thinking chain
• Parent gets a Telegram / email / webhook notification — no raw chat content shared
• Everything runs locally via Ollama — zero cloud dependency
